General

What is an FAQ section?

An FAQ section, or Frequently Asked Questions section, is a part of a website or document that addresses common queries or concerns users may have.

Why do FAQs matter?

FAQs matter because they provide quick and concise answers to common questions, helping users find the information they need without having to contact support or navigate through extensive documentation.

Where can I add my FAQ?

FAQs can be added to various places on a website, such as a dedicated FAQ page, product pages, or within specific sections related to services or policies. They should be easily accessible and well-organized to ensure users can find the information they are looking for efficiently.
